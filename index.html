About The Artist

Varsha

Varsha is a self-taught acrylic artist from Bangalore, India, whose creative journey began in 2021. What started as a personal escape quickly evolved into a passionate pursuit of translating emotions onto canvas through bold colors and expressive brushwork.

Her work spans portraits, nature studies, and abstract compositions—each piece reflecting her belief that art should evoke feeling before explanation. Varsha draws inspiration from everyday moments, cinema, and the quiet beauty found in ordinary life.

When not painting, she can be found exploring art galleries, experimenting with new techniques, or simply observing the world with an artist's eye—always searching for the next story to tell through her art.
